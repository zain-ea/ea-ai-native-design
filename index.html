Good to know

Questions, answered

Will comparing options affect my credit score?

No. Seeing your indicative options is a soft process with no impact on your credit score. We only do a formal credit check once you've chosen to proceed with a specific lender — and we'll always tell you before that happens.

How fast can I actually get funded?

Many approvals come back the same day, and funds can land in as little as 24 hours once you're approved and documents are signed. Larger or more complex deals can take longer — your broker will give you a realistic timeline up front.

What can I finance through EasyAsset?

Equipment and vehicles (new or used), business fit-outs, working capital, invoice factoring, trade finance and commercial property. If your business needs it to grow, there's likely a lender on our panel for it.

Do you charge fees?

We're upfront about cost. Any fees that apply to your finance are shown clearly before you commit — no hidden charges and no surprises at settlement.

How much can my business borrow?

Typically from $5,000 up to $500,000 and beyond, depending on your trading history, the asset and the lender. The quick tool above gives you an indicative range in seconds.

Do I need to bank with a specific lender?

No. We compare 50+ bank and non-bank lenders and match you to the right one for your situation — you're never tied to a single institution.
