Good to know

Marketplace questions, answered

How does getting quotes work?

Tell us what you're after — a category or a specific product — plus a few details about your business. We match your request to verified suppliers who respond with their best price and lead time, so you can compare quotes side by side. It's free and there's no obligation to proceed.

Is it free to use the marketplace?

Yes. Searching, browsing and requesting quotes is completely free for buyers. You're never charged to compare suppliers, and you're under no obligation to buy.

Can I finance what I buy?

Absolutely — that's the point of EasyAsset. Once you've chosen a supplier, you can finance the purchase through our panel of 50+ Australian lenders in the same place, often with funding in as little as 24 hours and no impact to your credit score to see options.

Which industries do you cover?

Six and growing: Industrial, Medical, Hospitality, Farming, Materials Handling, and Transport & Logistics — spanning more than 50 equipment categories and 50,000+ products.

Are the suppliers verified?

Yes. Suppliers on EasyAsset are vetted before they can quote, so the businesses competing for your order are genuine, established Australian operators.

What's coming next?

We're building EasyAsset into a full ecosystem for major purchases — Compare and Finance are live today, with Buy, Insure, Track & manage and Reorder rolling out so you can run the entire lifecycle of an asset in one place.
